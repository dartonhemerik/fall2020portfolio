Reflection

CSS Flex

Learning these two powerful layout methods were a huge boost to my productivity during the semester. Long gone are the days trying to structure my content with Float, which can be seen in the 'About' section of my previous COSW 10 web portfolio. The portfolio project you are currently on is made with CSS Flex and it's wrap property. Furthermore, the responsive design is made easier by adjusting the flex-basis.

Parallax

In week 9 I was really impressed by how much Parallax elevates a website's aesthetic with little CSS code. Naturally, I had to incorporate it into my final portfolio project, which you can see in the beginning and end. It takes a lot of playing around to get the text where I want it. Furthermore, for responsive design I made sure the font sizes within the parallax sections were changed too.

About

Hello there! I am currently pursuing an AS in Web Development at Long Beach City College. I hope to also attain a bachelor's and ultimately land a career in the web development field.

This is my second semester taking web development courses and I had the pleasure of being taught by Professor Laney in both COSW 20 - Front End Website Development and COSW 200 - Intro to JavaScript and jQuery. Having a full online course load was a new experience for me and I would be lying if I did not say there were some tough weeks. However, I am proud of how much I have learned in such a short period of time. My portfolio proves that.

Thank you for taking the time to check out my work. I really appreciate it.
